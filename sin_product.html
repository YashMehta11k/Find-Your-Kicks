Product Details


The Jordan 4 Retro SE Craft Photon Dust is a new shoe with Photon Treatment. Photon has already been released in Nike's Dunk, Air Max 90 and Air Force One models. For this pair, Jordan Brand modified the Air Jordan 4s' mesh cage, replacing it with soft, cracked leather cage wings. The Air Jordan 4 SE Photon Dust features a gray leather upper and is inspired by the second design in the Air Jordan line by Tinker Hatfield. TPU eyelets aslo gives this shoe a vintage aesthetic. Color Shown: PHOTON DUST/PALE VANILLA/OFF WHITE/GREY FOG/FLAT PEWTER/SAIL
